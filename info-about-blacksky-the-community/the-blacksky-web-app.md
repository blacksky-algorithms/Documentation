# The Blacksky Web App

This doc will be updated as new features are added.&#x20;

We are working on iOS and Android app implementation in the future.

**Last updated:** February 2026

***

## What is the Blacksky Web App?&#x20;

[Blacksky.community ](https://blacksky.community)is a web app (can only be accessed via a web browser) that assembles all the data on the network. Our web app has specific features that are listed below. <br>

***

## How to access the Blacksky Web App?

Go to [Blacksky.community](https://blacksky.community/) and log in or create an account. Once you are logged in, you will be able to immediately access your account, feeds, etc.&#x20;

You can also use our web app with any PDS. This means that if you host your own PDS or are even a Bluesky user, you can log into our web app with your credentials. Our moderation, community guidelines, and TOS still apply.&#x20;

{% hint style="info" %}
Currently, there may be pages that do not have blobs (profile images) and full follower counts shown due to backfilling. Backfilling is the process of syncing all the data in the network from scratch. At the time of the Blacksky web app launch, \~50% of accounts were synced. This process will take time, but you will still be able to use your account as usual. <br>
{% endhint %}

***

## Features

**Main Features:**

* Built-in moderation service (you do not have to subscribe to our moderation service via the web app). Bluesky data is viewable, but is under our moderation for the full web app, not just as a labeler or PDS moderation tool.&#x20;
* Blacksky feed is automatically built in.
* If your account is banned or suspended by Bluesky, our web app automatically copies that moderation decision, but if you send an email to [support@blacksky.app](mailto:support@blacksky.app) with your account information (if you are on our PDS), we will investigate the ban, and if it does not go against our TOS, you will regain immediate access to your account.&#x20;
* If you receive a label from Bluesky moderation, such as ‘rude’ or ‘sexual content’, it will appear on our web app, but you can appeal this via [support@blacksky.app](mailto:support@blacksky.app), and we will investigate it against our own moderation and remove the label on your account via our web app (if you are on our PDS).&#x20;

\
**Things to Remember**
----------------------

#### **Bookmarks, Drafts, and Mutes**

* Bookmarks, Drafts, and Mutes on the Blacksky Web App differ from your Bluesky app ones. You will need to redo these functions within the Blacksky Web App. This data is private and thus specific to which app you're logging into.

#### **Blocks**

* Blocks are universal across all apps. This means that if you had someone blocked on Bluesky, then they are also blocked on Blacksky.&#x20;

#### **DMs**

* If you have been banned from Bluesky but are unbanned in our web app, you do not have access to DMs because they are Bluesky-specific and closed-source. We encourage you to use [Germ](https://blacksky.community/profile/did:plc:4yvwfwxfz5sney4twepuzdu7) as an alternative to be able to talk privately with those on our web app.

{% hint style="info" %}
If you encounter any issues while using our web app, please send an email to [support@blacksky.app](mailto:support@blacksky.app).
{% endhint %}
